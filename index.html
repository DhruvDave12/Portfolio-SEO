Igniting Innovation: What is the Thiel Fellowship?

The Thiel Fellowship, founded by visionary entrepreneur Peter Thiel, is a two-year program designed to support young individuals who want to build new things instead of going to college. We believe that true innovation often happens outside the confines of traditional academia. Our fellows receive a substantial $200,000 grant, unparalleled mentorship, and access to a powerful network of founders, investors, and scientists in Silicon Valley and beyond.

This unique program provides the freedom and resources needed to transform ambitious ideas into impactful realities. It’s an alternative education path for future founders and aspiring innovators ready to challenge the status quo and make a tangible difference in the world.

Eligibility & Who Should Apply

The Thiel Fellowship seeks exceptional young talent. If you're passionate about building and creating, and ready to challenge traditional academic paths, this program is for you. We value ambition, initiative, and a clear vision for what you want to build.

  • Age: You must be 22 years old or younger when you are accepted into the fellowship.
  • Vision: A strong desire to build a new thing, whether it's a company, a non-profit, or a new movement.
  • Commitment: Willingness to prioritize your project over attending or continuing traditional college.
  • Innovation: A demonstrable track record of initiative, creativity, and problem-solving.

The Thiel Fellowship Application Process

Ready to join a community of world-changing innovators? The application process is designed to find individuals with drive, creativity, and a clear vision.

  1. Submit Your Initial Application: Share your background, your project idea, and why you believe the Thiel Fellowship is the right path for you.
  2. Interviews: Promising candidates will proceed to a series of interviews with our team and network members.
  3. Project Deep Dive: We'll explore the specifics of your project, your progress, and your plans for the two years.
  4. Final Selection: A small cohort of fellows is selected each year to embark on their entrepreneurial journey.

The Thiel Fellowship application process is rigorous, but it ensures we select the most driven and talented young founders. Get started on your application today!

Frequently Asked Questions about the Thiel Fellowship

What is the Thiel Fellowship?

The Thiel Fellowship is a two-year program for young people under 23 who want to build new things instead of going to college. Fellows receive a $200,000 grant and mentorship from a network of founders, investors, and scientists.

Who is eligible for the Thiel Fellowship?

The fellowship is open to individuals aged 22 or younger. We look for ambitious young innovators who are passionate about building and creating, and are ready to bypass or stop traditional higher education to pursue their projects full-time.

What kind of projects does the Thiel Fellowship support?

We support a wide range of innovative projects across various fields, including technology, science, entrepreneurship, and social impact. The key is that fellows are building something new and impactful.

Is the Thiel Fellowship worth it?

For young entrepreneurs and innovators eager to accelerate their ideas, gain significant funding, and access an unparalleled network, the Thiel Fellowship offers a transformative alternative to traditional college education, making it an incredibly valuable opportunity.
